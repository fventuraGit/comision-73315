/Bubbles;
Dive into the Bubbles experience: interactive spheres that gently float and react to your cursor’s movement, symbolizing the playful emergence of ideas. Their soft, translucent appearance adds a light, dynamic layer to the interface without overpowering the overall design.
/Liquid;
Discover the Liquid concept: smooth, flowing shapes that morph seamlessly across the screen, echoing the adaptability and elegance of modern web design. Perfect for highlighting key content transitions and creating an immersive, fluid user journey.
/Waves;
Experience the Waves element: rhythmic, undulating patterns that mimic the soothing motion of the ocean. This dynamic backdrop guides the viewer’s eye naturally through your content, infusing the page with balance and harmony.
/Smoke;
Explore the Smoke effect: delicate wisps that drift and swirl, evoking an ethereal, atmospheric mood. Ideal for adding depth and intrigue to your layout, drawing attention to featured sections with subtle, refined flair.
